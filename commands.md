# **Status-Bot**
## **Commands**
* melden (m)
    * raising hand ✋
* hoch
    * thumbs up 👍
* runter
    * thumbs down 👎
* reset (r)
    * reset your status
* resetall
    * reset everyones status
* help
    * display a help context
* clear
    * bulk the last 100 messages (that are under 14 days old)
